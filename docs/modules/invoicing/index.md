# Invoicing, Taxes & Discounts

Invoicing is where a sale turns into money owed — and into a tax-compliant document that both your customer and your government will accept. This module covers the everyday mechanics of getting an invoice right: how discounts and taxes are calculated, how prices and offers are applied, how customers pay (and how those payments get matched back to the invoice), and how the document is reported electronically to the tax authority in whichever country you operate.

If you raise sales invoices, run promotions and loyalty programs, reconcile customer payments, or are responsible for e-invoicing compliance, this is your module. The pages below are grouped by the job you're trying to do — start with discounts and pricing if you're configuring how invoices add up, jump to the e-invoicing group if your task is hooking Nama up to a national tax platform.
